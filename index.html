HIGHLIGHTS

REMA

Divine Ikubor, known professionally as Rema, is a Nigerian singer and rapper. In 2019, he signed a record deal with Jonzing World, a subsidiary of Mavin Records. He rose to prominence with the release of the song "Iron Man", which appeared on Barack Obama's 2019 summer playlist.

BUDDY

Buddy is an American rapper from the West Coast. He grew up in Compton, CA and gained recognition after releasing “Awesome Awesome” . After signing with Pharrell Williams, Buddy dropped his first mixtape, Idle Time, in 2014. It featured artists like Casey Veggies, Kendrick Lamar and CardoGotWings.

EARTHGANG

EARTHGANG is an American rap duo composed of Spillage Village-emcees Johnny Venus and Doctur Dot. Formed in 2008 in Atlanta, Georgia, EARTHGANG released its first EP, The Better Party, in 2010. This was followed by several singles and three albums, including Mad Men and Good News in 2011, Shallow Graves For Toys in 2014, and Strays With Rabies in 2015. Since being signed to J. Cole’s Dreamville Records in 2017, EARTHGANG has been hard at work, releasing three EPs, Rags in September 2017, Robots in October 2017, and Royalty in February 2018.
